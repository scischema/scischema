SciSchema is a collaborative, community activity with a mission to create, maintain, and promote schemas for machine-readable scientific methods, experiments, and processes.

SciSchema schemas can be used to describe scientific processes across many domains, including biology, materials science, chemistry, physics, imaging, psychology, and beyond.

The schemas support structured data extraction, semantic publishing, comparison, reuse, and integration with research knowledge graphs.

A shared collection of scientific process schemas makes it easier for researchers, data stewards, knowledge engineers, and tool developers to describe scientific methods in reusable and machine-actionable ways.

We invite you to browse the schemas or get started.
