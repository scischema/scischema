Machine-readable scientific process schemas

Making scientific methods persistent, reusable, and machine-readable.

SciSchema is a community-driven initiative to capture, structure, and share scientific methods as reusable schemas across scientific disciplines.

Inspired by the simple and community-oriented model of Schema.org, SciSchema provides human-readable schema pages, machine-readable schema files, and persistent identifiers for scientific process descriptions. Schema.org itself presents a shared vocabulary maintained through an open community process, with top-level navigation for Docs, Schemas, Validate, and About. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
